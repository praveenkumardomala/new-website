About Aditya Engineering College

Established with a vision to provide world-class engineering education, Aditya Engineering College has been at the forefront of technical excellence for over two decades. Our institution is committed to nurturing innovative minds and developing skilled engineers who can meet the challenges of the rapidly evolving technological landscape.

We offer state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and industry-relevant curriculum across multiple engineering disciplines. Our graduates are well-prepared to contribute meaningfully to society and excel in their chosen careers.

With a strong emphasis on research, innovation, and practical learning, we ensure our students gain both theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience that makes them industry-ready from day one.

Admissions

Join Aditya Engineering College

Take the first step towards a successful engineering career. Our admission process is designed to identify talented and motivated students who are ready to excel in the field of engineering.

Admission Requirements:

  • 12th grade with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Valid JEE Main/State CET score
  • Minimum 60% in qualifying examination
  • Age limit: 17-25 years

Important Dates:

  • Application Start: March 1, 2025
  • Application Deadline: June 30, 2025
  • Counseling: July 2025
  • Classes Begin: August 2025
